ASTRONOMY

Space is made up of millions of things like planets, stars, and galaxies. There is even a strong belief that there is other life out there, and there are always discoveries of planets and solar systems with similar atmospheres to ours. Read on to find out more about our solar system.

  • Mercury: Closest planet to the sun so it is very hot
  • Venus: The second brightest natural object in the sky
  • Earth: Our planet, on which 8.7 million different species live
  • Mars: The red planet, covered in sand and no water
  • Jupiter: The largest planet in the solar system and has 67 moons!
  • Saturn: Easily recognised because of its beautiful rings
  • Uranus: Referred to as an ice giant, it hits the coldest temperatures of any planet
  • Neptune: The furthest planet from the sun, very dark, cold and windy
  • Pluto: controversially regarded and disregarded as planet, we'll leave it up to you to decide!
